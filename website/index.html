About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Ritam Rana, a passionate engineer, tennis player and chess enthusiast.

Originally from the vibrant city of Orlando, Florida (where Disney World is), I am now embarking on a new chapter as I enter my freshman year at the University of Florida, where I will be pursuing a degree in both Computer Science and Statistics.

My love for the world of computers began at a young age, and I vividly remember the thrill of writing my very first line of code back in 5th grade. Since then, my skills have flourished, and I have honed my expertise in various domains. From delving into the intricacies of Computer Vision algorithms to programming and building robots that competed at the world championship, my repertoire spans a wide spectrum.

My most recent project was a Stock/Crypto price predictor bot, where I trained a Machine Learning model and derived sentiment scores of a stock through Natural Language Processing libraries to predict the next day open/close. You can check it out here!

Join me as I explore the ever-evolving landscape of technology, combining my technical prowess with an unwavering enthusiasm to make a positive impact.
